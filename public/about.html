Share. Earn. Learn.

Built by students for students, Ziras provides the expertise of a community of students to you when you need it.

It’s not always easy to get feedback on your assignments. Especially when professors aren’t available, peer review of your assignments can get you past a block. Ziras brings peer feedback to your fingertips.

Everyone has an expertise, but few are experts in all areas. Share your expertise and earn credit that you can use to get reviews of your work. Reinforce your own understanding of your field by helping classmates with less experience.

As we grow, we will see more and more disciplines represented, expanding even beyond the W&M community.
